Some useful points for good prediction



  • Upload Sudoku images with complete box , such that all four corner points are visible
  • Ensure the image doesnt have any such surrounding things which may disturb the extraction
  • PNG , JPG , JPEG images are only allowed
  • Ensure the grids are clear , and do not have any things ,writings or markings on them , as the system may consider them as a number
  • Avoid using images with watermarks on them.
  • Avoid using very colorful images
  • Crop the image again and again , before confirming such that no background comes into the main frame other than the sudoku grid as shown below

  • The solution may be wrong , if the cropped images has any of the above disturbances , if so try again with a more neat and clear image
